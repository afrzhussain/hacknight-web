About the event

HacktoberFest is a month long global celebration of open source software. It's the best opportunity to get into open source software development and explore the communities that help make a better, free and open culture in software industry.

We're celebrating hacktoberfest with a handful of workshops and an exciting hackathon to kickstart your creative hacking skills to build something cool in 12 hours with a team. This is a beginner friendly event to welcome budding new hackers to the world of open source software development and meet communities that keeps this motto alive.

2 Exciting Sessions

Session on some of the best topics including Actions On Google, Web VR and exciting info from open source and GitHub.

Overnight Hackathon

A 12-hour Overnight hackathon to enhance your programming and design skills. Meet new hackers and get mentored by experts from the industry.

10+ Mentors to help

Each of the tems will be assigned by a single mentor to help them through out the hackathon to build something cool based on given set of topics.

Surprise Challenges

Surprise Challenges to make the night even more exciting and challenging! Wait for the announcement midnight to take a break and grab some swags!
