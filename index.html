About the event

HacktoberFest is a month long global celebration of open source software. It's the best opportunity to get into open source software development and explore the communities that help make a better, free and open culture in software industry.

We're celebrating hacktoberfest with a handful of workshops and an exciting hackathon to kickstart your creative hacking skills to build something cool in 12 hours with a team. This is a beginner friendly event to welcome budding new hackers to the world of open source software development and meet communities that keeps this motto alive.

3 Exciting Workshops

Workshops on some of the best topics including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and exciting info from open source and GitHub.

Overnight Hackathon

A 12-hour Overnight hackathon to enhance your programming and design skills. Meet new hackers and get mentored by experts from the industry.

10+ Mentors to help

Each of the tems will be assigned by a single mentor to help them through out the hackathon to build something cool based on given set of topics.

Surprise Challenges

Surpris Challenges to make the night even more exciting and challenging! Wait for the announcement midnight to take a break and grab some swags!

Workshop on AI and ML

School of AI, MLG SOSC

An introductory workshop for beginners on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with practical modern applications. Workshop will cover basics and myths of AI/ML and spark the learning process by guiding through the resources to become a pro in AI/ML.

Workshop on BlockChain

UniCourt Mangaluru

ABeginner friendly introduction to the most advanced and hyped tech of this era! THe blockChain! from cryptos to highly scalable application, the session will cover basics of blockChain, How to get started and this will be the pilot session for Future workshops on BlockChain.

Intro GitHub and OpenSource
